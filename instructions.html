Instructions - Voting DApp

Usage Instructions:

  1. Connect MetaMask: Click the "Connect Metamask" button to connect your MetaMask wallet to the DApp.
  2. Ensure Network and ETH: Make sure you are on the Sepolia network and have enough ETH for transactions. You can redeem ETH from https://faucets.chain.link/sepolia.
  3. Register as Election Commission: If you are the election officer, click "Register EC" to set yourself as the Election Commission.
  4. Set Registration Time for Candidates: Set the date and time for candidate registration by filling out the form and clicking "Set Registration Time."
  5. Register Candidates: Candidates can register themselves by providing their information and clicking the "Register" button.
  6. Set Voting Period: Set the voting period by specifying the date and time, then click "Set Voting Time."
  7. Vote: To cast your vote, enter the candidate's serial number and click "Vote."
  8. View Results: Click "View Results" to navigate to the results page.
  9. Reset: Click "Reset" to clear all data and start over.

Security Notes:

  • Never Share Your Private Keys: Ensure you never share your private keys or any sensitive credentials with anyone. Your private keys provide access to your funds and should be kept secure at all times.
  • Avoid Hardcoding Credentials: Do not hardcode any sensitive information, such as private keys or passwords, directly into your codebase or documentation. This can lead to security vulnerabilities.
  • Use Wallet Providers Securely: When interacting with wallet providers like MetaMask, always ensure you are using the official version and verify the authenticity of any prompts or transactions before proceeding.
